Knorr Spinach Dip

  • 12 oz. frozen spinach
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 16 oz. sour cream
  • 1 package Knorr Vegetable Recipe for dips
  • 3 chopped green onions
  • 1 can chopped water chestnuts
  • 1 loaf of sour dough bread
  • 1 medium bowl

To starting making the dip, thaw spinach and drain. In medium bowl, mix mayo, sour cream, Knorr Vegetable Recipe, water chestnuts, and green onions.

Add in spinach and mix thoroughly.

Cut off top of sour dough bread (about top 1/3 of loaf) and scoop out bread.

Fill with the dip mixture and chill for two hours before serving.
